Simulator 01

Risk Analyzer

Paste a price series to generate core risk metrics. This is useful for evaluating a stock, strategy equity curve, crypto asset, or portfolio value series.

Why this matters

Businesses use this type of tool to monitor portfolio downside, compare assets, and quickly explain risk to stakeholders.

risk_report

Simulator 02

Options Pricing Lab

Price a European call using Black-Scholes, estimate the same option with Monte Carlo, and inspect key Greeks.

C++ build note

In C++, this becomes two modules: a closed-form Black-Scholes function and a Monte Carlo engine using std::mt19937.

options_report

Simulator 03

Portfolio Optimizer

Generate random portfolios for three assets and find the max-Sharpe and minimum-volatility portfolios. This is a simplified efficient frontier prototype.

Business use case

Useful as a prototype for allocation dashboards, investment committee tools, and internal portfolio analytics demos.

portfolio_report

Simulator 04

Strategy Backtester

Test a moving-average crossover strategy on a generated price path. Change the windows and transaction cost to see how fragile strategy performance can be.

Demand signal

Students and junior quants want credible backtesting projects because they demonstrate modelling, coding, and analytical judgment.

backtest_report

Simulator 05

Scenario Stress Simulator

Stress-test a portfolio by simulating future outcomes under different drift, volatility, and shock assumptions.

scenario_report
